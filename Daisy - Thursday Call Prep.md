## Probe Questions — Once in a Blue Moon Sale Walkthrough

### Setting up the offer
- Where do you write the copy? What tool, what doc?
- How do you decide what's in the offer — is that in your head or written down somewhere?
- Where does the payment link live — are you using HoneyBook, Stan Store, or Stripe direct?
- Is there a sales page, or does it go straight to a payment link?
- If no sales page — where does someone land when they click to buy?

### Sending it out
- Who gets this? Which list or segment?
- How do you select that segment — is it a saved filter or do you do it manually each time?
- Which platform are you sending from — Circle or HoneyBook? Why that one for this?
- Is the email written fresh or do you have a template?
- Do you post about it anywhere else (IG, YouTube community tab, etc.)?

### After someone buys
- What happens the moment someone pays — what do they receive?
- Is that automated or do you send it manually?
- Where does that person end up in your system — does HoneyBook know they bought? Does Circle?
- How do you track who's paid vs. who hasn't followed up?
- What do you do about people who clicked but didn't buy — do you follow up?

### Closing the sale
- How do you know when to close it?
- Do you do anything to close it — a "last chance" email, or does it just end?
- After it's done, where do you record what happened — how much came in, how many people bought?

### Edge cases (only if time)
- What if someone has a question before buying — where does that come in and how do you handle it?
- What if you're away or unavailable during a sale period?

## Things to Listen For

- Any step where she says "I'm not sure how it works" — that's a gap
