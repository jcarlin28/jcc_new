## HOW ENGAGEMENTS TYPICALLY WORK

Most clients start with either the Diagnostic Sprint or the Transformation Audit — a defined, time-limited engagement with clear deliverables. From there, many move into an Implementation Programme or Fractional Transformation Lead arrangement as the work develops.

This phased model means you're never locked into a long commitment before you know what you're getting. It also means the implementation is always grounded in real evidence — not assumptions about what your organisation needs.
